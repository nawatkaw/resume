What is CAPT?

On the way to Pokhara

CAPT, College of Alice & Peter Tan, is a residential college (RC) that provides University Town College Program (UTCP). A program that allows students to complete their general modules university requirements while enjoying their residential life here. CAPT promotes its mission of community engagement and active citizenship by providing a number of different platforms for students to learn through a living-and-living program (LLP). The living aspect of the LLP distributes the student population into five houses that are intentionally designed to provide an immersed and holistic university experience for the students.

Lesson Learnt

This journey has been a memorable ride. My partner and I contributed many ideas and initiatives to provide a sense of community and belonging for our Phoenix residents. Furthermore, from this role, I have learned many important life lessons from this experience, and I am glad to share 2 of my learning points from this role:

1. Never forget your initial motivation

There were times when I felt burnt out, or jaded from doing this role as there were many other responsibilities to take care of on top of this such as academics and family. However, I always reminded myself of my initial motivation to run as a House Head which was to create a sense of belonging for residents here to proudly call ‚Äúhome‚Äù. That reason motivated me to push on when times were tough.
